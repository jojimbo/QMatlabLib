calculateMarketEC

EC_GID = calculateMarketEC(obj, portfolio, scenarios, confidence, forex, reportCurrency, varargin)

This method calculates the Market Economic Capital for a particular portfolio. The user may specify which GID's to included.

Inputs:

  • portfolio Portfolio
  • scenarios ScenarioCollection
  • confidence double
  • forex ForeignExchange
